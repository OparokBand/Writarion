Biography of Sabbir Hosen Akash (Writarion)

Full Name: Sabbir Hosen Akash

Artist Name: Writarion

Date of Birth: January 3, 2002

Place of Birth: Sandwip Upazila, Bangladesh

Height: 5′7″ (1.70 m)

Profession: Singer-Songwriter, Composer, Lyricist, Writer, Music Producer

Early Life and Education

Sabbir Hosen Akash, widely known by his artistic alias Writarion, was born in the serene island town of Sandwip, Bangladesh. From a young age, he immersed himself in a musically enriched environment, developing a deep passion for melodies and rhythms. He became proficient in both the keyboard and guitar, showcasing early signs of musical brilliance.

He completed his SSC from South Sandwip High School in 2022 and is currently pursuing his intermediate education at Govt. Hazi A B College, with graduation expected in 2024.

Music Career

In 2021, Sabbir formed the Oparok Band, where he plays the keyboard and serves as a creative driving force. His compositions blend elements of pop, rock, and folk, creating a unique and emotionally resonant sound.

His debut album Mritto Bahok quickly garnered attention. Signature tracks such as "Mritto Bahok," "Moner Dukkho," and "Harano Golpo" reflect themes of love, loss, and personal growth. His powerful lyrics and melodies have made a significant mark in the contemporary Bangladeshi music scene.

Literary Works

In addition to his music, Sabbir is a rising figure in Bangladeshi literature. His debut book, Whispers of the Heart, explores love, self-discovery, and inner transformation. His poetic writing style explores human emotions just as deeply as his music.

Notable Achievements

  • Founder and Lead Keyboardist of Oparok Band
  • Multiple-time Winner of the Sandwip Music Program
  • Released his first solo song in 2022 (self-written, composed, and performed)
  • Recognized for captivating lyrics and evocative compositions
  • Verified Artist on Spotify and Apple Music

Future Goals

Sabbir envisions a future where AI integrates with artistic expression. He is passionate about creating intelligent systems that combine music, literature, and futuristic technology—pushing the boundaries of human imagination.
